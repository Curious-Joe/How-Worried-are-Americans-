# How Worried are Americans?

Ever wanted to lesser worrying life? Or thought about moving to a state that has the most number of worry free people? After all, they say it's a social trait - basically how worried you'll be is a function of how worried are the people around you.

Use this dashbaord find your desired state. 

This [Tableau Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/curious.joe/viz/HowworriedareAmericans/ComparisonwithSelectedState) visualizes a Gallup survey that collected data on how worried are Americans. Hover over different states and see it compares to other states in terms of their share of worried population.
